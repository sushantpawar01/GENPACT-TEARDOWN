## Client Personas

### 1. Anita Sharma (46) - CFO, Manufacturing Enterprise
* **Bio:** Anita oversees global finance operations. Her challenge is controlling costs and maintaining visibility over multi-entity financial processes.
* **Pain Points:** Month-end close takes 10+ days. Manual reconciliations and data errors. No predictive forecasting.
* **Goals:** Automate reconciliations, improve forecast accuracy, and reduce finance cost by 30%.

### 2. Rahul Verma (40) - VP Supply Chain, FMCG Company
* **Bio:** Rahul manages procurement and logistics across 30 countries. He struggles with delayed shipments, poor visibility, and inefficient vendor management.
* **Pain Points:** No end-to-end supply chain visibility. Reactive rather than predictive planning. High inventory and logistics costs.
* **Goals:** Create a real-time “control tower.” Optimize transportation and procurement spend. Reduce logistics costs by 20%.

### 3. Maria Lopez (38) - Head of Customer Experience, Banking Sector
* **Bio:** Maria oversees a large contact center. Customer satisfaction is declining due to inconsistent service and slow response times.
* **Pain Points:** Long resolution times. Lack of unified customer view. Reactive complaint handling.
* **Goals:** Achieve 95% First Contact Resolution. Integrate data across channels. Use AI for proactive service.

## The Problem: Core Pain Points

* **Data Fragmentation:** Finance, supply chain, and CX data exist in silos.
* **Manual, Redundant Processes:** Overreliance on spreadsheets and human approvals.
* **Poor Predictive Insights:** Limited analytics or real-time dashboards.
* **Reactive Operations:** Lack of early warnings for issues.
* **Rising Operational Costs:** Inefficiency in routine transactions.

---

## Proposed Solutions

### Solution 1: Intelligent F&A Transformation
* **Target:** CFO Anita (Slow close cycle, manual reporting)
* **Implementation:** Implement Genpact Cora Finance—a unified AI-led platform. Automates reconciliations, journal entries, and reporting. Provides real-time dashboards.
* **Impact:** Month-end close reduced to 3 days. Forecast accuracy ↑ 25%. Cost per transaction ↓ 40%.

### Solution 2: Supply Chain Control Tower
* **Target:** VP Rahul (No visibility, high logistics costs)
* **Implementation:** Deploy Genpact Control Tower, integrating data from ERP, WMS, and logistics partners. AI predicts risks like delays and suggests alternatives.
* **Impact:** On-Time, In-Full (OTIF) ↑ 20%. Transportation cost ↓ 18%. Inventory turnover improved by 15%.

### Solution 3: AI Customer Experience Hub
* **Target:** Head of CX Maria (Poor CX consistency, reactive service)
* **Implementation:** Genpact Cora CX Platform integrates voice, chat, and CRM data. GenAI assists agents with real-time recommendations and sentiment detection.
* **Impact:** First Contact Resolution (FCR) ↑ 30%. Average Handle Time ↓ 20%. NPS improved by 25%.

### Solution 4: Human + Digital Workforce (RPA)
* **Target:** General Operations (Automation stops at exceptions)
* **Implementation:** Cora integrates bots with human experts. Exception management routed automatically to domain specialists.
* **Impact:** 70% straight-through processing. SLA adherence ↑ 40%. 24x7 seamless operations.

## Process Evolution

### As-Is Finance Process (Manual)
1. Data entry and validation done manually.
2. Emails for approvals and reconciliations.
3. Reports prepared in Excel.
4. Delayed month-end close and errors.

### To-Be Finance Process (Digitally Optimized)
* **Automated Data Capture:** RPA bots capture and validate data from all sources.
* **Real-time Dashboards:** Managers get immediate visibility into P&L and variances.
* **AI-driven Reconciliation:** Cora AI automatically reconciles accounts and flags only true exceptions.
* **Predictive Analytics:** Forecasting models are run in real-time to predict outcomes.
* **Continuous Improvement:** Genpact Cora monitors process health and suggests further optimizations.

### To-Be Supply Chain Flow
1. Orders captured from all channels (EDI, web, manual).
2. Data instantly synced across ERP, WMS, and Transport systems.
3. AI predicts potential risks (e.g., port delay, stockout).
4. Control Tower dashboard alerts the operations manager to the risk.
5. Manager reviews AI-suggested alternative (e.g., reroute shipment).
6. Customer is notified of the change proactively, in real time.
